About Me

Are you excited to hear a story about transformation?

Well, let me introduce myself - most people know me as "Danny", a Python Developer. But my path to this career was far from straightforward. For over a decade, I worked as an Accountant. However, as time passed, I found myself irresistibly drawn to the dynamic and creative world of software development.

I discovered a passion for problem-solving, logical thinking, and the flexibility and versatility of Python as a programming language. Through Python, I found the opportunity to work on projects that have a direct impact on people's lives, and I knew that this was my true calling.

So, despite being in my early 30s, I made the bold decision to take a leap of faith and transition into a new career as a Python Developer. And I have to say, it was one of the best decisions I've ever made.

Now, I'm on a mission to create solutions that make a difference, using my skills and expertise in Finance and Python.
